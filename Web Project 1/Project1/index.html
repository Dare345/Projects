Intro

With the uprising popularity in AI technology, there is no doubt life will become a lot simpler and easier. As they learn, they will be able to learn certain skillsets that will most likely impact our daily lives. Despite all the positive aspects AI can have on people, there are also drawbacks to it. Its ability to replicate human creativity is terrifying and can become an issue for certain fields in the future. People like artists and animators are possibly in danger due to generative ai being capable of replicating any art or film quicker than any human. With that in mind, there are possible opportunities for generative ai to be used and implemented in another way.
