Generative AI's Automation

Its ability to replicate human creativity is terrifying and can become an issue for certain fields in the future. People like artists and animators are possibly in danger due to generative ai being capable of replicating any art or film quicker than any human. With that in mind, there are possible opportunities for generative ai to be used and implemented in another way.
